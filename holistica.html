About Holistica

Our Holistic Approach

At Holistica, we believe in treating the whole person, not just symptoms. Our bio-psycho-social model integrates biological, psychological, and social factors to provide comprehensive mental healthcare.

We're committed to making mental healthcare accessible, affordable, and effective for everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

Important Disclaimer

Holistica is equipped to provide care and support for individuals experiencing psychological distress, including complex psychiatric disorders and other conditions. For those in need of more intensive care and daily support, we are launching an in-patient care facility in Bengaluru soon.

If you or someone you know is experiencing at-risk behaviors or safety concerns, or any other life-threatening crisis or critical mental health situation, contact a helpline or go to the nearest hospital or emergency room. Having a close family member or friend with you for support can be invaluable during this time.

For emergency mental health support, please call the national Tele MANAS helpline at 1-800 891 4416.
